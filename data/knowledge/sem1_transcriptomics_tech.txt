19 SEM1 24/25 BIS7019-B – ‘Omics’ Techniques in Biology and Healthcare
***Recommendations based on guidance given by Illumina
Experimental considerations:
• 50 bp single-end reads generally sufficient for
standard gene expression profiling, and may
only need 5-25 million reads per sample
• Identification of novel variants, splice sites or isoform
analyses can benefit from longer (e.g., 75-100 bp)
paired-end reads (ideally stranded) + greater read
depth (e.g., 30-60 million reads per sample)
• For small RNA analysis (e.g., miRNA), a single read
(usually 50 bp) typically covers entire sequence, with most
applications ranging from 1-5 million reads per sample
