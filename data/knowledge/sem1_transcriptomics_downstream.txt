15 SEM1 24/25 BIS7019-B – ‘Omics’ Techniques in Biology and Healthcare
Defining ‘statistical significance’ of the difference:
What do I mean by ‘statistical significance’?
• Calculate a P-value associated with difference in expression
For each gene:
• Significant difference when P-value < threshold (e.g., 0.05)
• ↓ P-value = ↑ likelihood there is a ‘real’ difference
Differential expression analysis
16 SEM1 24/25 BIS7019-B – ‘Omics’ Techniques in Biology and Healthcare
Defining ‘statistical significance’ of the difference:
• Thousands of genes = thousands of statistical tests
Does this present any problems?
• Inflates chance of incorrectly concluding there is a difference
• Need to adjust raw P-values to minimise number of ‘false’ discoveries
