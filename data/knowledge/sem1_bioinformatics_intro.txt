What is Bioinformatics? Informatics 

Biology 
Study of living things and  their vital processes 
Study, design and development of  information technology that generates,  stores, processes, and presents information 

Bioinformatics 
Using computer technology to collect, store, analyse and disseminate  biological data and information (e.g., DNA and amino acid sequences)
